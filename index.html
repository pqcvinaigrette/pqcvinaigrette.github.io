Cryptographic scheme

Vinaigrette,
a finely blended threshold scheme

A tangy mix of structure and randomness for modern privacy-preserving protocols.

Post-quantum Multi-party friendly

Vinaigrette is a cryptographic threshold scheme built like a perfect dressing: a delicate emulsion of triplets. Carefully balanced randomness keeps secrets dispersed, while verifiable structure ensures every drop can be checked.

Use it as the core ingredient in privacy-preserving authentication, anonymous credentials, or bespoke protocols where you need rigorous proofs without leaking the recipe.
